Inspiration

NarrArtive was inspired by our shared love for storytelling and the belief that collaboration among people from diverse backgrounds can create captivating narratives. Growing up in extended African families, we witnessed the rich tradition of collaborative storytelling as a significant part of our leisure time. We realized that this art of storytelling should not be forgotten. Our project is a testament to the power of combining words and images to unite and craft stories that transcend individual boundaries. It is owed to a passion for inclusivity, diversity, and the celebration of individual voices.
